TEAM

 

Grupo Enesa’s leadership team is known for its deep training and for its experience in managing companies in the energy and health markets in which the fund participates in.

JAIME CHICO PARDO

Founding Partner, Chairman and Managing Director of Grupo Enesa.

 

Jaime Chico Pardo is a former  Chairman of the Board of Telmex, Carso Global Telecom and Ideal. From 1995 to 2006 he filled the position of Managing Director of Telmex. Before he joined the Telmex team, he was Managing Director of Grupo Condumex, Euzkadi/General Tire de México, and Fimbursa. He built his career in the international banking industry with Banamex and started an investment bank, IFI de México, in 1984.

 

Today, Jaime Chico Pardo is a board member of Honeywell International, AT&T and Chicago Booth GSB.

 

He holds a degree in industrial engineering from Universidad Iberoamericana.

CARLOS CÁRDENAS BLÁSQUEZ

Today, Carlos is Managing Director of Enesa Energía, he participates on the investment committee, and he sits on the Board of Directors.

 

Carlos Cárdenas was part of América Móvil for more than 12 years. During this time he worked as Chief Operations Officer and Operations Director for Latin America. Before joining América Móvil, he held several positions in Telmex, including Operations Director at Buscatel and Vice-President of Operations at Telmex USA. He is also a former Director at Grupo Financiero Inbursa.

 

He holds a degree in industrial engineering from Universidad Iberoamericana.

SANTIAGO CHICO SERVITJE

Santiago Chico is currently Managing Director of Vsys and Director at Grupo Enesa. At Enesa, he is part of the investment committee and sits on the Board of Directors.

 

Santiago served as an investment analyst at Grupo Financiero Inbursa and was co-founder of PCFIX. He has also held positions with Philipp Morris, JPMorgan, América Móvil, Condumex and Prodigy USA.

 

He holds a degree in technology and telecommunications engineering from Universidad Anáhuac and he earned a MBA from Stanford Graduate School of Business.

ROLANDO ZÁRATE ROCHA

Managing Director of Hubard since 2001, today he is a Director of Grupo Enesa and PROCURA.

 

Rolando Zárate worked for 30 years for Ingenieros Civiles Asociados (ICA) and became Managing Director of Urban Construction and Managing Director of ICA Puerto Rico.

 

He graduated from Universidad Nacional Autónoma de México with a degree in civil engineering and holds a Diploma in Senior Business Management from IPADE.

GABRIEL HAJJ

Gabriel Hajj is currently a Director at Grupo Enesa, where he participates on the investment committee. He also sits on the Board of Directors of Enesa Energía.

 

Gabriel is also co-founder and served as Managing Director at Play Telecom. He also worked with The Boston Consulting Group in Mexico and the United States. He worked in strategic planning at Grupo Modelo and before that at Satmex.

 

He holds a degree in industrial engineering from Universidad Anáhuac, where he graduated at the top of his class, and he also holds an MBA from Stanford Graduate School of Business.

JOSÉ JAVIER VARGAS CAÑADA

Javier Vargas is currently Grupo Enesa’s Technology Director. For five years, he was Director of Operations and Co-Founder of the unified communications service company VSYS, which was acquired by Telmex in the first quarter of 2014.

 

Javier worked at Hewlett-Packard for ten years, assumed different leadership responsibilities in the Software and Consulting business units. He also worked at Teléfonos de México for eight years, where he participated in the creation, development and operation of data networks and Internet services.

 

Javier has more than 20 years of experience in the Information Technology and Telecommunications sector, with extensive experience in innovation processes, in quality and continuous improvement, as well as in technology management´s best practices and standards that apply to strategies in the private, public and government sectors.

 

He has served as a professor in special master’s degree programs at the Instituto Tecnológicos de Estudios Superiores de Monterrey and Universidad Iberoamericana, as well as giving presentations at various forums on technology-related issues.

 

He received his degree in cybernetics engineering from La Salle University and holds an EMBA degree from IPADE.

IÑIGO GUTIÉRREZ

Iñigo Gutiérrez is currently Operations Director at Laboratorio Médico Polanco.

 

Iñigo has spent the last 12 years within the health and pharmaceutical industry. He is a former partner of an investment bank that specializes in pharmaceuticals, as well as a former Director of Finance, Administration and Operations at several healthcare industry companies, including chains of laboratories, optics stores, and retail pharmacies.

 

He has been involved in more than 30 M&A transactions during his career and has been director and financial representative of companies listed on the Mexican Stock Exchange and the NYSE.

 

Iñigo began his career in corporate banking at Banamex, and in commercial banking at Bancomer. Afterwards, he worked for six years at Transportación Marítima Mexicana, where he became Finance Director.

 

He received his degree in industrial engineering from Universidad Anáhuac in Mexico City and has received graduate-level training in economics and business. He also holds a master’s degree in economics and finance from the University of Warwick in England.

ALEJANDRO RUÍZ

Alejandro Ruíz is the Managing Director of Laboratorios Ruiz.

 

Alejandro is a Level III National Researcher of the CONACYT´s National System of Researchers, Regular Member of the Mexican National Academy of Medicine, and Full Member of the Mexican Academy of Science.

 

He is a former vice-president of the Scientific Division of the International Federation of Clinical Chemistry and Laboratory Medicine, and a former member of the Scientific Committee of the International Society for the Advancement of Cytology.

 

He received his medical degree from Universidad Autónoma de San Luis Potosí and became an Immunology Specialist at the Mayo Graduate School of Medicine/University of Minnesota.

GERARDO MUÑOZ

Gerardo is Chief Financial Officer at ENESA Energía, sits on its investment committee, and on its board of directors.

 

He was involved in the international expansion of América Móvil from 2000 to 2014, where he served in the roles of CFO and CEO in three countries: Ecuador, Colombia, and Chile.

 

Gerardo was also responsible for Investment Relations in Telmex from 1995 to 2000, and worked in different finance positions in Grupo Condumex from 1988 to 1995.

 

He holds his Bachelor’s degree in Actuary from Universidad Anáhuac, and a graduate degree in Direction of Operations from Instituto Tecnológico Autónomo de México (ITAM).
