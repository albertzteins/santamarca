ENESA NEWS

Grupo Enesa is a private capital fund that invests in and manages companies in the energy and health sectors in Mexico and Latin America. Its leadership team consists of a group of professionals who have extensive experience in operations and finance, as well as a broad knowledge of the industries and markets in which the fund participates. The fund operates under a long-term strategy that is focused on building solid, high potential organizations.

 

Jaime Chico Pardo / Chairman and Founder of Grupo Enesa

Jaime Chico Pardo was Chairman of the Board of Telmex, Carso Global Telecom and Ideal. From 1995 to 2006 he held the position of Managing Director of Telmex. He is also a former Managing Director of Grupo Condumex, Euzkadi/General Tire de México, and Fimbursa.

  • Enesa Energía allies with Invenergy.

    Enesa Energía is creating a strategic alliance with Invenergy, a company that develops and operates power plants in the United States, Canada, and Europe. Together they will develop energy projects in Mexico.

  • Energy in biogas and biomass.

    Enesa Energía and Green Gas develop power generation projects using biogas and biomass.

  • Selmec arrives to Tultitlán.

    Selmec starts up its plant in Tultitlán, State of Mexico, one of the most modern plants of its kind in the Americas. With an area of approximately 10,000 m2, the plant will improve the quality of its products and increase its production capacity fivefold.

  • PEMEX chose Enesa's consortium.

    Petróleos Mexicanos chose the consortium consisting of Enesa Energía, Invenergy, Clean Power, and Mexichem to develop the Cactus Cogeneraton project. The project will involve building a power plant and an associated transmission system that will have a preliminary capacity to generate 530 megawatts and 500 tons of steam per hour.
