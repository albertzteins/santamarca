ENESA ENERGÍA

 

Enesa Energía’s vision is to provide the most complete solutions in energy efficiency. We supply everything from engineering to construction, operation, and maintenance of thermal power projects and high-, medium-, and low-voltage electricity, as well as backup systems. Each of the group’s companies is noted for its years of experience, its leadership and the world-class standards employed in its operations.

 

SELMEC

We have more than 70 years of experience providing comprehensive solutions for electric and steam power.

 

• Sale and leasing of power plants ranging from 10 to 2,500 kW.

 

• Electrical systems include: substations, panels, transformers, UPS, A/C, and more.

 

• Maintenance and remote monitoring for steam and electrical equipment with 24/7 nationwide support.

 

www.selmec.com.mx

CLEAVER BROOKS

Complete boiler systems.

 

• We offer industrial steam or hot-water boilers, with either water-tube or fire-tube technology.

 

• We are manufacturers, and the only authorized dealer in Mexico (for more than 50 years) of Cleaver Brooks and Nebraska water-tube and fire-tube steam boilers ranging from 10 to 30,000 HP.

 

• Our selection includes boilers, burners, controls, waste heat recovery units, parts, and water systems.

www.cleaver-brooks.com.mx
