ENESA SALUD

 

Grupo Enesa’s Healthcare division provides a balance between routine and specializaed services in healthcare. Enesa Salud, through its member companies, offers the widest variety of testing, starting with predictive tests. Decades of experience make it possible to properly treat more than 85,000 patients per month in central Mexico.

LABORATORIO MÉDICO POLANCO

 

This diagnostic laboratory has 34 years of experience offering clinical and diagnostic analytical services in Mexico City and the metropolitan area. Its more than 700 employees receive in excess of 70,000 patients per month. It has two pathology laboratories and an imaging interpretation center.

www.lmpolanco.com

LABORATORIO Y CLÍNICAS RUÍZ

 

This diagnostic laboratory, with more than 60 years of experience, has been recognized on at least 20 occasions both nationally and internationally. Located in the city of Puebla in Mexico, it currently serves more than 15,000 patients per month. It is the first laboratory in Mexico and Latin America to have the Beckman Coulter AU5400, a unit capable of processing more than 4,000 tests per hour.

 

It offers everything from routine services to genome tests. It is a pioneer in applying pharmacogenomics in bioequivalence studies.
